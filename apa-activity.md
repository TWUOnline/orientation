# APA Style

The MA Leadership program uses the _Publication Manual of the American Psychological Association \(6th ed\)_ for formal papers. You are strongly encouraged to purchase this book to use as a guide as you write your papers.

There will not be any formal training or required readings from the book, but your instructors understand that you are learning to engage in a scholarly community and will be happy to assist you with specific questions.
