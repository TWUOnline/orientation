Photo Safari:

I decided to do this at home one afternoon. I live in a very small basement suite and I actually thought it would be a bit challenging because of the limited space. It was fun to look at my space a little differently. Someone else&#8217;s hand is hard to photograph when you live alone so I took a picture of a hand on my TV. Human emotion is a picture of my parents when they were 18 years old at a friends wedding (they have been married 40 years now).
