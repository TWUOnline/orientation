<h3>Photosafari:</h3>
<p>It was very difficult to think through</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>my shots with the 15 minute time frame. I was thinking of the <a href="https://create.twu.ca/orientation/digital-literacy/1000-words/"><strong>get pickier</strong></a>, and realized that most of my shots had very little in the way of deep thought to them. I did this at work in my classroom. Being the excellent teacher that I am, I did not read all the instructions before writing my blog post and thus the formatting may be off as I did not post the pictures as a gallery. Next time I am posting photos I will put them in a gallery as the layout is far more appealing.</p>
